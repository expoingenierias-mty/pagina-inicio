INVESTIGACION Y DESARROLLO DE PROPUESTAS DE MEJORA

Automated Production Line at Smart Factory

The Smart Factory at Tec has a numerous amount of new technologies, being one of them a conveyor system. This conveyor system has presented issues with its components and configuration for the past months. The objective of this project is to practice reverse engineering in the existing system, to fix all these issues and to make the conveyor work properly again.
