A convolution neural network for automatic skin cancer classification using smartphone images

A deep learning model that is trained with an existing dataset that consists of clinical images taken with different smartphone cameras. The way that the user will interact with the web application will be by simply taking a picture with a smartphone of the skin area that they want to analyze and uploading it to the application. This would allow them to evaluate their patients’ skin lesions and abnormal skin areas in order to make more accurate recommendations to their patients concerning whether they should visit a dermatologist for further inspection or just keep the skin lesion under observation. This would serve as a way to prevent the further progression of a skin lesion that has the potential of becoming malignant.
