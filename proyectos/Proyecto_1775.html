DESARROLLO DE PROTOTIPO FISICO

Pibotino

Robots and drones are already a big and important part of modern industry, and we can already see them beginning doing basic jobs in our civil lives, like last mile delivery, informational stands or being an extra eye for the user. The Pibtino can be seen as the second version of the already existing Pibot, which can be seen as a research and service robot for various applications. Equipped with multiple cameras and sensors, Pibot makes it possible to collect a huge amount of data of its environment and process them directly. Pibotino is now the second version of this robot, attacking mechanical problems and implementing new functions and provides an expandable platform to support research projects of the future.
