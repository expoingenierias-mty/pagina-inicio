Assessing Urban Accessibility: A comparative analysis between the Tec District, Monterrey and the Phoenician Triangle, Bogotá

To develop sustainable cities, it is necessary to change the way cities are traditionally planned and designed. Therefore, this study shows a comparative analysis of urban accessibility between the Distrito Tec, Monterrey, Mexico and the Triángulo Fenicia, Bogotá, Colombia. This project can help decision-makers to make informed decisions based on highly useful and relevant data to improve citizen's quality of life.
