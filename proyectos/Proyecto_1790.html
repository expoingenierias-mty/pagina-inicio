Model-based Design for an Electric Bus: Electrobus

The objective is to create a simulation platform for an electric bus capable of generating valuable information to enable a model-based design methodology for the electrification of the chassis. This platform shall enable a user to experiment with the virtual vehicle under several conditions, environments and with diverse parameters to test and validate design concepts. This will generate information for data-driven decision-making in the design process of a sustainable mobility solution such as Electrobus.
