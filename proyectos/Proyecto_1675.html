INVESTIGACION Y DESARROLLO DE PROPUESTAS DE MEJORA

Biomechanics for the Digital Twin of Performance

Using CV algorithms to track human’s movement, in addition to biometric devices that measure biomechanical features, to create a deep learning model to predict biomechanical features without the devices themselves, and thus create an easy-to-use tool further used in geriatric healthcare to predict risk of fracture or to measure an athletes’ performance.
